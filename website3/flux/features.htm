Messaging Center

Our messaging center offers a brand new way to keep in contact with all your co-workers. With this, you can always keep in contact and know what's going on. With the ability to send messages from the web, mobile app, or even through text message to a group, easy communication is always at your disposal

Brand new to this feature is the ability to link in all your facebook and mobile friends so you have everyone you'd want to talk to all in one place. Just select which group of contacts you'd like to choose from and talk away.

Notifications

We make it easy to stay in the loop no matter the circumstances. With our notification system, you can receive reminders about shifts, changes to the schedule, requests for shift changes, messages from management and so much more. We offer text message notifications that you can reply to through text as well. No matter the situation, we have the solution to always be notified.

Through the settings menu, you control what notifications you get. If you're the kind of person who always wants to know what's going on, just select all the options. On the other hand, if you only like to receive those crucial updates, we make that possible too. With Flux Planner, it really is all up to you.

Storage

Flux Planner offers cloud storage! This means no matter where you go, your files can be with you. With 5Gb of storage space, you can store all your important documents and files, or even movies and music. We offer the space, you choose what goes in it.

We also offer constant upgrade ability. If you find yourself in need of more storage space, a quick one time charge will be applied and your back to having more with you on the go.

Travel Manager

Getting to work can sometimes be a hassle. Flux Planner allows you to connect with your fellow employees and see if anyone can offer a lift or if anyone needs a lift. It works by matching living proximity with who's schedules match up.

Flux Planner also has traffic reporting to let you know if you have to leave a bit earlier, as well as bus times and schedules that get you to work on time.

Searching Made Easy

Our search bar makes finding what you want to do super easy. All you have to do is type in the function you want to do or person you want to see, and the results come up.

Let's say you want to email John Jackson from work. Just type in mail @JohnJackson into the search bar and it brings your straight to your message thread with him. This is the same for many other functions. We make simplicity easy to find.

Docufile Editor

With DocuFile, you can have access to all your documents anywhere you go. If you have a presentation for school and need to fix a powerpoint mistake, or have a proposal for a manager that you just found a typo in, DocuFile brings file editing on the go

You can edit your files and then save them directly to your cloud. Then when you need them again you can bring it right back up. We also make sharing easy. You can email your files, transfer via WiFi, or remotely print them out. We bring all the technology to your hands.

We know sharing is caring. Just select which group or individuals can view your files, and they can access it from wherever they are. This brings new abilities to teamwork.

Your Stats

We make it easy to keep track of all of your stats. If your connected to your company through Flux Planner, all the information about your sales, hours, and more are all in the palm of your hand. This is great for sales competitions, hour tracking, and simply knowing how you preform within your company.

Even if you're not hooked up through your work, Flux Planner has a simple punch-in, punch-out system that easily allows you to track all the hours you work.

Multiple Jobs

We know that managing more than one job can be quite difficult. All the schedules, reminders, contacts that you have to keep track of. We make life easier.

We give you a place where you can have access to all of your information all in one place. Choose your schedules, manage your notifications, and find all your contacts. We make what is otherwise a messy hassle, a simple click away.
