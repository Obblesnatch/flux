Flux Planner

Your day-to-day Planner

Our Planner is a key component to our package. Whether you're an employee or part of management, our planner can make your life much easier.

Employees

It's through our Planner that you can be able to fully control your shifts.

We've worked in the service industry so we know the stress that can come from last minute changes. We make it incredibly easy to switch up your shifts

Here's how it works!
If you want to:

  • Bid off a shift
  • Request a shift
  • Switch a shift
  • Request a day off
  • Or posting your availability

Flux Planner makes it just a click away

Furthermore, Flux Planner gives you a notification for all replies and requests so you never have to worry. Also built into the Planner notifications are shift reminders so you always know when you have a shift

Management

For managers, Flux Planner is the ultimate tool in shift creation. You outline your shift rules such as the required workers per shift and required hours and Flux Planner narrows down your options. This makes your life a whole lot easier. You just drag and drop your available options into the timeslots, click save, and you're done!

You can choose to view each individuals schedule for the week or all your employees

Mutual features

The best part about Flux Planner is that it's so much more than just a work organizer. Life is stressful and keeping track of plans is rough. With our planner you can put all your events, meetings, deadlines and reminders all in one place right beside your work schedule

New to Flux Planner is the ability to add your facebook events to your schedule so you have a central hub for all your important events
